About me

  • Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Aarhus University, Denmark
  • Ph.D. (Dr. rer. nat.) in Computer Science, University of Konstanz, Germany
  • Former Research Assistant at the Human-Computer Interaction Group, University of Konstanz, Germany
  • Former Senior Software Developer at ionCube Ltd., Canterbury, UK

Highlighted Research

My research combines human-computer interaction, mixed reality, and visual analytics. My work focuses on the field of hybrid user interfaces, which use cross-device interaction with complementary device technologies. In addition, my work on ambient analytics aims to bring calm computing to mixed reality by integrating immersive data visualizations harmoniously into the physical environment.
